Panda Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Panda ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our Android application ("Panda" or the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. By using Panda, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide
We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the core features of Panda. However, if you choose to sign in with Puter.js for enhanced features, we will collect your Puter.js account information as described in their Privacy Policy.

b) Voice Data
When you use Panda's voice features, we temporarily process your voice input to convert it to text for command interpretation. This audio data is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties beyond what is necessary for the voice recognition service.

c) Device Information
We collect general device information such as your Android version, device model, and app version to ensure compatibility and improve our service.

d) Usage Data
We collect anonymous usage statistics such as feature usage frequency and session duration to understand how people interact with Panda and improve the user experience.

e) Task Execution Data
To perform tasks on your device, we temporarily access the information necessary for each specific task (e.g., contact names for messaging, app names for launching). This data is processed locally and not stored.

f) Screen Content
When you request screen analysis, we capture and process the visual content of your screen to understand context and provide relevant responses. This data is processed locally and not stored.

g) Log Data for Debugging
To diagnose and fix issues, we log some interaction data with our third-party AI services. This data is sent to Puter.js key-value store and includes the AI prompt, the AI's response, the AI model used, and success/error status. This log data is not linked to your personal identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain Our Service: Including processing voice commands, executing tasks, and analyzing screen content as requested.
  • To Improve Our Service: Using anonymized usage data and debugging logs to enhance app performance, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • To Communicate With You: Sending important updates about the app, though we currently do not collect email addresses for direct marketing.
  • To Comply With Legal Obligations: Retaining certain data where required by law.

3. Third-Party Services

Panda integrates with several third-party services to deliver its functionality:

  • Google (Gemini AI Models): Your conversations, screen content (XML or screenshots), and text from new memories are sent to Google's Generative AI services for processing, reasoning, and analysis. This is essential for the AI to understand context and generate responses. Your data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Puter.js (Key-Value Store): Anonymized log data of AI interactions is stored in Puter.js key-value store for debugging and service improvement, as described in section 1(g).
  • Tavily (Search API): If a task requires web research, a search query derived from your instruction is sent to the Tavily Search API to find information. This is subject to Tavily's Privacy Policy.
  • Puter.js (Authentication): If you choose to sign in, your authentication is handled by Puter.js. This is subject to Puter.js's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with partners for research and improvement purposes.

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Panda, our users, or others.

5. Data Retention

We retain debugging logs and usage data for a period of 90 days, after which it is automatically deleted. Personal information you provide (if any) is retained only as long as necessary to provide our services.

6. Children's Privacy

Panda is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will promptly delete it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
